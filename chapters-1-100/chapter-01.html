1.2 The Basic Formula

The core of the system is surprisingly simple:

‚û§ Aim point = Departure point ‚Äì Arrival point

This formula is used for all shots where:

  • You play via the long rail
  • The route then goes to the short rail
  • The departure point is numerically higher than the arrival point

The system only works if these conditions hold.

1.3 Diamonds and Reference Points

To work with the Basic Diamond System, the player must become familiar with three values:

  1. Departure (D) ‚Äî value of the cue ball position
  2. Aim (A) ‚Äî the diamond you must aim at
  3. Arrival (R) ‚Äî place where the ball must arrive on the third rail
